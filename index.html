Northern Michigans Largest Independent SUP Dealer!

Crystal Lake Adventure Sports, located in Beulah is Northern Michigan’s premier source for sales, service and rentals of stand up paddle boards, kayaks, canoes and bicycles. Plus, we have all the gear, clothing and accessories that go with them. Our shop in Beulah is just a block and a half from both beautiful Crystal Lake and the Betsie Valley Trail, our new 22-mile hiking and biking trail. Come on in and we’ll help you get started, or keep you going with your love affair with the great outdoors and with adventure sports. Come visit our rental kiosk 1 block from lake Michigan in Frankfort.
